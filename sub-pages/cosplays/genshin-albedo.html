Why I Cosplayed Albedo

I bought the Albedo cosplay to have another cosplay to wear during the 2022 Cherry Blossom Festival. At the time, I only had my Evelynn, Thresh, and Thoma cosplays to choose from.

Crafting Process

I bought the cosplay on Miccostumes, which was sufficient for the cosplay. The chains definitely felt heavy, making me use bigger safety pins to attach the chains to the cosplay. The cosplay showed signs of wear after the Cherry Blossom Festival, and since I wore parts of it for more casual hangouts with friends and cousins, I ended up selling the cosplay. Albedo was the most worn out cosplay I ever sold to this day.
