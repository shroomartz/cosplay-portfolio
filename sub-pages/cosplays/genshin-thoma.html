Why I Cosplayed Thoma

I initially cosplayed Thoma as I thought his cosplay would be great for a casual cosplay. I could wear the crop jacket, dog tag, red hip sashes, and black shirt with my other non-cosplay clothes like olive slacks. I tended to wear the Thoma cosplay for cosplay and non-cosplay purposes, including to a dinner at an omakase place in my city. The restaurant appreciated how sharp I looked.

Crafting Process

I bought the Thoma cosplay and wig through AliExpress, though I never used the wig and kept it in order I ever needed to do the full cosplay. I never had to use the wig, allowing me to go around with the full cosplay like it was some kind of flashy outfit, which it was.

My Thoughts

My Thoma cosplay was the gateway to me using parts of my cosplay to wear outside of cosplay scenarios. The cosplay parts were outlandish enough to look like special, stylish outfits to the public eye.
