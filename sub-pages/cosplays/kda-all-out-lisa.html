Why I Cosplayed Lisa

Lisa and Evelynn share the same English voice actor (Mara Junot), similar vibes to their character, and a similar demeanor. Combining the two together in a shiny popstar garb felt like a cool way to showcase the sleekness of both characters.

Crafting Process

I used spare iridescent spandex that I had lying around from my attempt at a handmade K/DA ALL OUT Evelynn jacket to make the leg decorations, belt, and everything that’s considered K/DA. I liked how I was able to bring new life to what would otherwise be a ½ yard of fabric laying around just looking pretty.

My Thoughts

I had a decent idea of the decorations I wanted to take from the K/DA outfits (Seraphine and Ahri’s side chain belt, Evelynn’s jacket, Seraphine’s leg wraps in her Rising Star form) to translate it to Lisa. I also liked being able to reuse a formal long black dress I wore for prior dances and conventions to make the concept of K/DA Lisa come to life.
