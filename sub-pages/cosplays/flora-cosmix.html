Why I Cosplayed Flora

After selling my Enchantix cosplay, I wanted to continue using my Flora wig.

Crafting Process

I bought the fabric around my city, and although I did not use the optimal fabric for some parts, I was able to somewhat recreate her Cosmix form. Sewing took 1-2 weekends.

My Thoughts

I didn't really like wearing this cosplay as I felt like I was coming home after participating in a Nick Jr show. It was a gimmick that only really worked for Halloween (where I wore the cosplay) and I eventually donated the whole cosplay, save for the skirt, wig, and heels. I used the wig and heels for future Flora cosplays and I used the skirt for Star Guardian Sona.
